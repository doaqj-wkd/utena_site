Utena County

Utena is an industrial city. It is known for its clothing, food and beverage factories. In recent years, however, streets, public squares and large areas of the parks in the city were reconstructed and Utena is now a more attractive for recreation and tourism.

Utena is located in northeastern Lithuania. The city covers 15,1 km² and is the 8th largest city by area in Lithuania. Four rivers cross the city territory: Vyžuona, Krašuona, Vieša and Utenėlė. There are also two lakes in Utena, Dauniškis and Vyžuonaitis.

Beautiful Lakes

Dauniškis is a lake located in Dauniškis park, Utena city, Lithuania. It has a length of 0.7 km and maximum width of 0.3 km. There's school and church near it, making it a beautiful scenery for everyone wanting to have a quiet and relaxing day.

Activities

Quiet relaxation is not the only way to go in Utena. Camp "Žygeivvių slėnis" offer classes for archery, kayaking, you can go on hikes and even have a stay overnight in the trees. It's really interesting way to spend your day. They also offer:

  • cognitive camps
  • a stone age barrier trail
  • stony golf
  • a ridge of obstacles
